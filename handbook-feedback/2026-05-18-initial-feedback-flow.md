# Feedback: Initial Feedback Flow

- Handbook page: https://aiweb3.school/zh/handbook/
- Type: structure suggestion
- Source note: `daily/2026-05-18.md`
- Status: draft

## Problem

The Handbook encourages open contribution and feedback, but a new learner may not immediately know how to turn a learning question into a structured feedback item.

## Suggested Change

Consider linking a lightweight feedback template from the Handbook contribution section. Suggested fields: page link, issue type, problem, suggested change, why it matters, and source learning note.

## Why It Matters

A standard feedback shape makes learner questions easier to index, triage, and convert into Handbook issues or pull requests.
