# Daily Note: 2026-05-21

## Topic

AI x Web3 two-way fusion.

## Summary

The core frame today was mutual complementarity: Web3 can provide decentralized incentive and verification structures for AI, while AI can reduce Web3's interaction complexity and strengthen proactive risk analysis.

## My Takeaway

The phrase "off-chain computation, on-chain scoring" is useful. It separates expensive intelligence production from public incentive settlement. That pattern can also apply to legal/compliance intelligence markets.

## Programmable Compliance Connection

Intent-centric wallets and autonomous agents make compliance design more urgent. If a user can express intent in natural language, the system must define:

- what the AI may infer;
- what it may prepare;
- what requires explicit user confirmation;
- what requires jurisdiction or risk review;
- what must be logged for later audit.

## Follow-Up

- Map intent-centric wallet workflows to compliance checkpoints.
- Explore whether legal/compliance intelligence can be scored through a subnet-like market.
